About

This tool visualizes the semantic relationship between individual patent claims, Orange Book use codes, and approved label indications for FDA-approved drugs. The trap score measures the gap between how well patent claims match use codes vs. how well label indications match use codes. A positive trap score suggests patent scope may exceed approved indications.

GAT view shows the bipartite graph between individual claims (left) and use codes (right), with edges weighted by cosine similarity. Claims are grouped and colored by patent.

Model: S-PubMedBert-MS-MARCO (768-dim). Threshold: 0.88 cosine similarity. Data: 2,927 NDAs, 228K patent claims, 17.8K label indications.
