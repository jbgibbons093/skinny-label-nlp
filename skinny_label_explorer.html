About

This tool visualizes the semantic relationship between patent claims, Orange Book use codes, and approved label indications for FDA-approved drugs. The trap score measures the gap between how well patent claims match use codes vs. how well label indications match use codes. A positive trap score suggests patent scope may exceed approved indications.

Model: S-PubMedBert-MS-MARCO (768-dim). Threshold: 0.88 cosine similarity. Data: 2,927 NDAs, 228K patent claims, 17.8K label indications.
